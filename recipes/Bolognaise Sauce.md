Great simple meat sauce for spaghetti bolognaise or lasagna. 
## Ingredients

1. 1lb ground beef
2. 1 onion
3. 3 cloves garlic
4. 1 can diced tomatoes (15.5 oz)
5. 1 tbsp tomato paste
6. Beef stock (15.5 oz)
7. Red wine (1/2 cup)
8. 1 tbsp Oregano
9. 2 bay leaves

## Instructions

1. Soften onion in olive oil - around five minutes.
2. Add garlic and continue to cook - around one minute. 
3. Add beef and brown.
4. Add tomato paste and stir one minute.
5. Add tomatoes, beef stock and red wine. 
6. Add 1tbsp oregano, 1tsp salt, 1tsp freshly ground black pepper and bay leaves.
7. Simmer for 1 hour.
