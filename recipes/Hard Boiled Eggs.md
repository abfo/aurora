Delicious hard boiled eggs without any grey gunk around the yolks. 
## Ingredients

1. Eggs (as many as needed)
##  Instructions

1. Place eggs in a pan and add water to cover. 
2. Bring the water to a slow boil.
3. Turn off the heat and put a lid on the pan. 
4. Wait ten minutes.
5. Drain the water and put the eggs in an ice bath.
6. Wait ten minutes.
